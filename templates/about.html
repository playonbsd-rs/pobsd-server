Game summaries, storylines and most of the images are provided by IGDB.

Game status

Here is the meaning of each status:
  • Doesn't run: speaks for itself.
  • Game launches: not enough information to comment meaningfully on status beyond launching the game.
  • Major bugs: potentially game-breaking, making finishing the game impossible or a chore; noticeably degrading the enjoyment compared to running the game on other platforms.
  • Medium impact bugs: noticeable, but not game-breaking.
  • Minor bugs: barely noticeable, or not relevant to core game.
  • Completable: game can be played through until the credits roll, without major bugs (category 2); doesn't (necessarily) include optional side content, DLC, optional multiplayer, achievements etc.
  • Perfect: the complete game including optional content like DLC, side quests, multiplayer can be enjoyed.

If you think that the status of a game is incorrect or need to be updated, please do not hesitate to report it at https://github.com/playonbsd/OpenBSD-Games-Database, at @thfr@bsd.network or on #openbsd-gaming (IRC).

You want to help?

If you own one of those games, then play a bit with them and report how far you went. If you own one of those games, then any information regarding playing them on OpenBSD is of interest.
